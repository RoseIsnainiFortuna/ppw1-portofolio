They often include technical skills such as knowledge of software, tools, or processes, and can also encompass proficiency in languages or other specialized areas. Hard skills are essential for performing job-specific duties and are typically quantifiable, making them easier to demonstrate to employers.

Softskills

Soft skills are traits and behaviors that influence how you interact with others and perform in the workplace. Unlike hard skills, which are technical and measurable abilities like coding or data analysis, soft skills are intangible and often relate to emotional intelligence, communication, and teamwork. They are essential for building relationships, resolving conflicts, and navigating complex work environments.
